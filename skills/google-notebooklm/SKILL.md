## What is NotebookLM?

Google NotebookLM is an **AI research assistant** that lets you upload documents, websites, videos, and other sources, then ask questions, get summaries, and generate insights grounded in YOUR sources. It uses Gemini as the underlying model.

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Source Upload** | PDFs, Google Docs, websites, YouTube URLs, text |
| **Q&A** | Ask questions answered from your sources |
| **Summaries** | Auto-generate summaries of uploaded content |
| **Study Guides** | Create study guides from documents |
| **Audio Overview** | Generate podcast-style audio discussion of your sources |
| **Citations** | Answers include citations to source material |
| **Notebook Sharing** | Share notebooks with collaborators |

## Best Models for Motion Control Research

When researching "what model is best for motion control," use NotebookLM to:

1. **Upload sources**: Add research papers, blog posts, comparison articles about AI motion control models
2. **Ask specific questions**:
   - "Which AI model has the best motion control for video generation?"
   - "Compare Veo, Kling, Seedance, Runway, and Pika for motion accuracy"
   - "What are the strengths and weaknesses of each model for camera movement?"
   - "Which model is best for dance motion generation?"
3. **Get cited answers** grounded in the uploaded sources

## Steps

1. Login to Google (use encrypted vault)
2. Navigate to `https://notebooklm.google.com/`
3. Click **"New Notebook"**
4. Upload sources (PDFs, URLs, paste text)
5. Ask questions in the chat
6. Use **"Notebook guide"** to generate summaries and study guides
7. Use **"Audio Overview"** for podcast-style discussion

## Notes
- NotebookLM grounds answers in YOUR sources — it won't hallucinate outside the uploaded content
- Great for comparing models: upload comparison articles and ask targeted questions
- Audio Overview creates a 2-host podcast discussing your sources — useful for quick overviews
- Sign-in requires Google account — TOTP must be in vault for automation
