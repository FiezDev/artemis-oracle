**Change:** TaskNet upgraded to v3.0 with new organizational structure.

**Chain of Command:**
```
CEO → Artemis → Division Lead → Division Agent
```

**Division Leads:**
- Atlas 🗺️ — Developer (Forge, Pixel, Shield, Deploy)
- Iris 💎 — TikTok (Luna, Sage, Vox, Nova, Reel)
- Herald 📰 — Facebook (Echo, Pulse)
- Axiom ⚖️ — Improvement (Prism, Spark, Metric)

**Key Changes:**
1. Artemis assigns tasks to Division Leads (not individual agents)
2. Division Leads do detailed planning for their domain
3. Artemis ALWAYS routes improvement data → Axiom
4. Axiom runs cronjobs for data collection and research

**Reporting Requirements:**
- Every 15 min during active task
- When claiming task
- When task ends

**Why:** Clearer responsibility boundaries and better coordination flow.
**How to apply:** All task assignments follow the chain. Division Leads are the point of contact for their domains.
